While I love many animals, the one there is one that is always especially close to my heart. Trixie is a mutt that we rescued from a shelter, and one of my favorite things is to watch her run around outside or ask for treats!

She's a bit of an older dog, so sometimes bringing her on walks can take between 30 minutes and an hour, and she's sometimes a bit crazy and does things I don't understand, but I always love her regardless and look forward to seeing her tail wag day after day.
